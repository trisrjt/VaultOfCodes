Chocolate Cake

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 30 mins

Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • 1¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1½ tsp baking powder
  • 1½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Steps

  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease two 9-inch round pans.
  • Mix flour, cocoa, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a bowl.
  • Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla; beat until smooth.
  • Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin).
  • Pour batter into pans and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  • Cool for 10 minutes, then remove from pans and cool completely.
  • Frost as desired and serve.
